Baby Bok Choy


Description

This is a delicious dish. It is an authentic Chinese recipe that I use for my cooking.


Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauc
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons brown sugar

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix together the oyster sauce, soy sauce, and brown sugar until the sugar has dissolved. Mix the potato starch and water in a small bowl, and stir it into the oyster sauce mixture until smooth.
  2. Prepare dumplings: Mix pork, garlic, egg, chives, soy sauce, sesame oil, and ginger in a large bowl until thoroughly combined.Fill a pot with water, bring to a boil, and stir in salt and vegetable oil. Place the bok choy into the boiling water, and cook until tender, shiny, and bright green, 2 to 3 minutes. Drain the bok choy, and arrange attractively on a serving platter.
